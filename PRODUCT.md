# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Competitive tennis players and their support network — college players, serious club players, coaches, and parents tracking junior athletes. These are not casual players; they're actively working to improve and gain a competitive edge. They come to the dashboard seeking confidence in their data — clear, trustworthy numbers that validate their effort and reveal where to focus next.

## Product Purpose

Advantage Analytics turns raw SwingVision match data into actionable performance analytics. Players upload `.xlsx` exports and get statistical breakdowns, court visualizations, shot-by-shot analysis, and AI-powered match commentary. Success looks like a player checking the dashboard before practice and walking onto court knowing exactly which pattern to drill.

## Brand Personality

**Modern. Athletic. Innovative.**

Built by former collegiate players, Advantage Analytics is a premium tool for serious competitors. It should feel exclusive, like a pro-level training room, not a consumer app built for mass adoption. Every pixel should communicate "this was made by people who understand the game at a high level." The interface earns trust through precision, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- **SwingVision** — too consumer / mass-market, designed for everyone.
- Any app that hand-holds, gamifies, or treats the user as a beginner.
- Playful illustrations, rounded bubbly UI, achievement badges, streak meters.
- Bounce animations, glassmorphism, neon accents, gradient-heavy surfaces.
- Warm tones, browns, earthy palettes, decorative color.

## Design Principles

1. **Data speaks first** — Every layout decision prioritizes legibility and comprehension of match data. No ornamental elements that compete with numbers.

2. **Earned trust through precision** — Aligned tabular numbers, consistent spacing, exact token usage. Players trust tools that feel meticulously crafted.

3. **Quiet confidence** — The interface doesn't shout. Light font weights, subtle borders, restrained color. Confidence comes from clarity, not volume.

4. **Pro-level exclusivity** — Design for the player who knows what a second-serve percentage means. No hand-holding UI, no dumbed-down explanations. Density is acceptable when it serves understanding.

5. **One accent, one purpose** — Blue (#3B82F6) means action or emphasis. Green (#5DB955) means winning / positive. Red (#E51837) means losing / negative. No other semantic colors. No decoration colors.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **Target**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- **Color contrast**: All text meets 4.5:1 ratio (normal text) and 3:1 ratio (large text) against backgrounds.
- **Focus indicators**: Visible focus rings on all interactive elements (`ring-2 ring-[#3B82F6]/40`).
- **Motion**: Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — skip transforms, keep opacity.
- **Keyboard**: All interactive elements reachable and operable via keyboard.
- **Screen readers**: Semantic HTML, ARIA labels on icons and custom controls.
